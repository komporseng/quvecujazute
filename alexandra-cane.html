    Professional Life

    Alexandra Cane is a professional makeup artist, social media influencer, and occasionally a model. She originally found her passion for makeup when she was just a teenager. Being a trained dancer, every competition, show, and demonstration was another opportunity for her to try another beautifully striking hair and makeup look.

Alexandra’s work has also been featured in The National Wedding Show, Oxford Fashion Week, and London Fashion Week.
