    Who is Adin Boyer?

    Adin Boyer, 22, is an Orange County, South California, native. He graduated with BFA from CalArts in 2021. He gained widespread international notoriety as a music performer and composer from CalArts. After graduating, he released his debut EP titled 'Caeload', which is a series of songs. He wrote songs and overwhelmed the internet with his beautiful voice. Adin is not new to music. His songs are influenced by Coldplay, Kensington, and Keane, and glorious groups like Hammock and Bear's Den. Moreover, Adin is well-versed in the classical piano choir, opera, and music theories. Adin is an indie rock artist.

    The star rose to fame during the Covid pandemic. The five-song collection of his album was recorded by his band of CalArts comrades. His website claims that the album was engineered, mixed, and mastered by Robert Adam Stevenson of 'A Silent Film' at Revolver Recordings. His music videos racked up millions of views on TikTok in 2020. The website claims that the singer received appraisal from big-name musicians such as Lizzo, Clairo, Colleen Ballinger, and Charlie Puth.

    As of now, Adin is working on his new songs on his sophomore EP. His current project will help him honor and amplify fellow artists and colleagues with disabilities. Adin's new EP will be released in 2023.

    Adin Boyer in 'American Idol' Season 21

    Adin claims that 2023 is the "most prominent year" of his life. He uploaded a series of photos showing his attire for the audition day at 'American Idol' Season 21 Week 3's episode. The singer will be seen in a purple shirt, accessorized with a blue tie and blue pants. He captioned  his post, "You’re about to witness me and some other epic individuals take one of the biggest high-stakes gambles in Las Vegas (and New Orleans and Nashville), auditioning for American Idol in front of the celebrity judges!" 

    Adin claims that performing at 'American Idol' was the "opportunity of a lifetime" for him. The singer claims, "I carried everyone’s spirit with me at this monumental audition and I am massively looking forward to sharing it with the world. The best is yet to come." As per his posts, it seems that the star will be performing indie rock songs in the audition round.

    Let us further wait to see whether the budding singer will manage to bag a golden ticket.

    'American Idol' Season 21 Episode 3 will premiere at 8 pm ET on Sunday, March 5, 2023, on ABC and the next day on Hulu.
