    Earlier today, Jen Harley took to social media to tell her followers that she really misses being in love! This comes two days after her on again, off again boyfriend and baby daddy, Ronnie Ortiz-Magro revealed that he was in rehab for one month for alcohol abuse and depression.

    So did Jen shade Ronnie or is she suggesting that she’d like to start anew with him after his rehab stint?

    A reunion between the two is definitely not out of the question considering how many times they just got back together despite shocking fights that even got the police involved.

    Harley took to her Instagram account to share the ambiguous message that reads: ‘I miss sleeping all over and being in love and having a best friend.’

    Alongside the caption, there was also a pic of a neon sign reading ‘love me.’

    Now, the fans are convinced that she is trying to mend things between her and Ronnie.

    Interestingly enough, she previously called him out for canceling on their Valentine’s Day plans to go on a dating show instead, only to delete the post.

    ‘Sorry but if you go away for six weeks to become a better boyfriend, father, person, to work on your family but you cancel all of our Valentine’s Day plans so you can show up on a dating show because you need the money that bad over your family…you are a joke. How did you better anything? You continue to put us in the same position by doing the exact same things that put us there in the first place,’ she wrote.
