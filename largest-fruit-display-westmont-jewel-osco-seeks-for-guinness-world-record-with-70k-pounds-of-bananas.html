    WESTMONT, Ill. (WLS) — A grocery store in suburban Westmont is going for a Guinness World Record Wednesday.

    The Jewel Osco at N Cass and Ogden avenues has put together what could be the largest display of bananas the world has ever seen.

    They say it’s about 70,000 pounds of bananas.
