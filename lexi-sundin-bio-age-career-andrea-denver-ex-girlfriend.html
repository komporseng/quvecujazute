    Is Lexi Sundin Andrea Denver Girlfriend/Ex-Girlfriend?

    Yes. Andrea may have confirmed that he reconciled with his ex-girlfriend Lexi Sundin and it is she that we are now going to talk about.

    During an interview with the outlet in February 2022, the Bravo TV personality confirmed that he now has a girlfriend. “I can’t spoil too much, but I’m dating someone, and I’m really happy” he shared.

    Andrea only gave a clue that his romance was related to Season 6 of the Bravo series. “It’s kind of related to my last summer” were his words before “I can’t say much”.

    Though Andrea did not disclose the girlfriend’s name, speculations circulating online were all pointing at the same thing, that he is back together with his Lexi, his ex-girlfriend.

    Andrea had mentioned Lexi on Summer House when he called Mya the name Lexi by mistake.

    Meanwhile, in recent times, Andrea was seen leaving heart emojis and complimentary comments on Lexi’s Instagram posts.

    With Summer House still going on, we now may know how it ends: with Paige and Craig as one couple and Andrea exploring a relationship with someone else.

    It is believed that Andrea was single when he first went to the Hamptons for the Summer House. Things are thought to have changed since filming wrapped up in the summer of 2021.

    Lexi Sundin Career

    Like Andrea, Lexi is a New York-based model. She has been signed to AMP Models and Ursula Wiedmann Models.

    Lexi appeared in a total of seven episodes of the Project Runway TV series back in 2019. While the fashion designer Jhoan Sebastian Grey took home the crown of the season (17th), Lexi later shared that she felt privileged to wear a few of his designs on the show as his model. “Each time it was a dream🥰 He knows how to make a woman feel beautiful in his pieces. @iamsebastiangrey I cannot wait to see what you do next with this major accomplishment”, Lexi had congratulated the Istituto Marangoni Miami alum for the win.

    If you also should get a hint at Lexi’s salary, she should have made an estimated $35.83 on an hourly basis working as a model in New York, New York, as per indeed.com.

    Her rumored boyfriend, on the other hand, is an Italian model. Andrea signed with Wilhelmina Miami towards the end of 2013 and also signed with Soul NYC. He got renowned, especially after his “net victim” portrayal in Taylor Swift’s “Blank Space” music video.
