    Martyn LeNoble's Early Life

    Christina Applegate's husband, Martyn LeNoble, was born in Vlaardingen, Netherlands, on April 14, 1969. Born into a musical family, LeNoble was greatly inspired and attracted to the beauty of music.

    Martyn's mother played Keyboards, while his father was skilled in playing Saxophone. As a child, Martyn began playing guitar but later turned to bass.

    In 1978, LeNoble, with only $75 in pocket, moved to Los Angeles alongside his family. He was introduced to different music styles that mesmerized him. Martyn then started working on his skills and landed a gig with the band Thelonious Monster in the mid-1980s.

    Martyn LeNoble Song & Music Ventures

    As mentioned, Martyn LeNoble's music venture started at a young age. At the age of 14, he was already a bassist in the Dutch band Punk Rock.

    After a couple of gigs, LeNoble founded his band, Porno for Pyros, in 1992. He was joined by Peter DiStefano, Stephen Perkins, and Perry Farrell.

    The alternative rock band released several songs, including Black Girlfriend, Pets, Cursed Female, and so on. However, in 1998, the band broke up, and its members went on to pursue other projects.

    LeNoble has released some solo songs. They are She Sells Sanctuary, Been Caught Stealing, Mountain Song, etc. In his successful music career, Maryn has worked alongside the cult, Daniel Lanoise, Sarah McLachlan, and others.
