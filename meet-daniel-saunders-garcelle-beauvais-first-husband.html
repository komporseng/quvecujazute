    Meet RHOBH Star Garcelle Beauvais First Husband, Daniel Saunders

    Garcelle Beauvais met her first husband Daniel Saunders when she was modeling in New York. The two immediately fell in love once they saw each other. Two months later, they got married. The couple was married from 1991 to 2000.

    Although Oliver acted in some TV shows as a child, he had his heart set to become a rapper from a young age. Oliver entered the Hip-Hop industry and started making music under the moniker Jayson Rose.

    “Blunts and Starbucks” was Oliver’s debut mixtape. He has opened for The Roots’ Questlove and shared stages with Talib Kweli, 9th Wonder, and Dom Kennedy.

    Amid the coronavirus pandemic, Oliver tied knots with his longtime girlfriend Samantha Saunders. Garcelle broke the joyous news as she congratulated the couple on their marriage.

    A month before the wedding, Oliver and Sam had welcomed a son named Oliver Jr. By the time they tied the knot, they were parenting four children already.

    Is Daniel Saunders Married Today?

    In the year 2000, after Garcelle separated from Daniel, she found love with producer and manager Mike Nilon. Garcelle and Daniel had many infertility problems and after five years of treatment, they have birth to twins Jax Joseph Nilon and Jaid Thomas Nilon in 2007.

    After nine years of happy marriage, Garcelle filed for divorce as she found out that her husband was cheating on her with his co-worker for five years.

    One morning when Garcelle was using Mike’s phone to call her mom, she found a text from Mike’s co-worker saying “I love you.” After she found out he was cheating on her, she exposed his affair in an email that she sent to his coworkers with the title “What [do] Tiger Woods, Jesse James, and Mike Nilon have in common?”

    However, in an interview many years later, Garcelle revealed that she regrets acting so childishly in anger and fury. The former couple is on good terms these days though as they co-parent their twin sons.
