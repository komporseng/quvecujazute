    Turkish Drama Sanam Sana Aur Sadia Story

    The story of the Turkish drama Sanam Sana Aur Sadia revolves around three sisters who got separated from each other when they were very young. A day before Sanam’s (Sevda Erginic) wedding day she comes to know about her sisters. She tries to contact them and invites them to her wedding.

    Her sisters come to meet her on her wedding day. They remain shocked when they come to know that Sanam is marrying a man who is her father’s age. On Sanam’s wedding night when everyone leaves. His husband starts fighting. She calls her sisters for help. They come to rescue Sanam from her husband. He tries to kill Sanam and falls from the roop while fighting and dies.
