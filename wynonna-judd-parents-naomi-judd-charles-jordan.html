    Wynonna Judd was born Christina Claire Ciminella, and she mistook her father for Michael Charles Ciminella, her sister Ashley’s biological father, for many years (birth name: Ashley Tyler Ciminella).

    However, Judd tells Andy Cohen on his Sirius XM show that she found out in the early 1990s that her father was a man named Charles Jordan, who was Naomi Judd’s boyfriend and left after Naomi became pregnant with Wynonna.

    Wynonna Judd parents: Naomi Judd, Charles Jordan

    Her biological father was never met by the country singer.

    She tells Larry King in a 2005 interview that she had planned to meet him about a month before his death in 2000, but never got the chance.

    Naomi Judd and Michael Ciminella divorced in 1972, leaving Naomi to raise Wynonna and Ashley as a single mother.
